about

Hi! I'm Sydney.
I'm currently working in a research group at a hedge fund. I'm interested in markets, effective altruism, progress and the long-term future. This website is a catch-all for things I'm writing, projects I've been working on, and other miscellaneous stuff. You can read a little about my philosophy for building a personal website here.

background

I studied math, economics and philosophy at the University of Arizona, where I was also awarded a FLAS Fellowship for advanced study in Arabic. Before starting college I worked on several organic farms around the northeastern U.S. where I learned a lot about growing and cooking excellent food. My first job out of college was in a macro-finance research group at the Federal Reserve Bank of New York, and before that I interned at NASA Langley Research Center for a year.

things I think about a lot:

  • market microstructure (a.k.a. how the rules of markets, especially financial markets, impact participant behavior)
  • progress studies (a.k.a. what are the conditions under which technological progress happens and how can we replicate them)
  • effective altruism (a.k.a. what do we value most and how can we maximize that value)
  • how financial institutions and markets interact with economic growth and technological progress
