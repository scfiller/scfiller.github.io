I'm Sydney!

past

I graduated college in 2021 with degrees in math, economics and philosophy. Growing up, my family moved around frequently, but I spent most of my life in Mons, Belgium and Tucson, Arizona.
I was raised Catholic but became an atheist after I was confirmed around age 13. Before starting college I took a gap year to work on organic farms around the northeastern U.S., where I met a bunch of Quakers, decided to convert and turned a gap year into a gap two years.

present

I no longer consider myself a Quaker and have a less straightforward relationship with religion now, but I find myself drawn to the history and influence of Christianity and Judaism on Western history and enjoy reading and thinking about religion seriously (I really, really like C.S. Lewis).

I'm broadly interested in what people online call "progress studies", especially the history of economic thought and the evolution of financialized economies. I live in NYC and currently work as a researcher at a hedge fund, which I really enjoy.
